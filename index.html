About me

My name is Alex Chiang (you can call me Alex). I’m from Taipei, Taiwan, where I enjoy the mix of city energy and nearby mountains for weekend hikes.

I’m pursuing a Master’s degree and focusing on areas related to computer science and network security. This term I’m taking a hands-on Network Attack and Defense course to connect theory with practical lab work.
